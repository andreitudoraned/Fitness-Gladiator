TRX Suspension Training

TRX Suspension Training requires only two adjustable bands to workout every single musncle in your body. TRX workouts do not only improve your muscle strength but also your cardiovascular endurance. This type of workout is also low impact which means joints are not put under much stress which results in very low risk of injury.
